How it works

  1. We ignore spaces and non-letters, then cancel matching letters between the two names.
  2. We add the remaining letters: count.
  3. We repeatedly count around F L A M E S, remove one letter, and rotate the list until one remains.

For entertainment only. Different variants exist; this matches the common "cancel letters + rotate" method.
