How it works

  1. Spaces and non-letters are ignored. Matching letters between the two names cancel each other out.
  2. The remaining letters are counted to get a number.
  3. We count around F · L · A · M · E · S, removing one letter each round, until one remains.

For entertainment only. This uses the common "cancel letters + rotate" variant.
